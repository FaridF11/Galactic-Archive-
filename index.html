The foundation

What Star Wars Is About

Mythic family drama

At its center, Star Wars follows families, mentors, apprentices, and chosen identities under the pressure of galactic war. The Skywalkers are the main spine, but the saga constantly widens to ordinary pilots, rebels, senators, clones, smugglers, mystics, and citizens.

The Force

The Force is an energy field generated by living things. Jedi seek harmony, restraint, compassion, and defense. Sith pursue domination through fear, anger, and attachment twisted into possession.

Politics and empire

The Republic falls through corruption, emergency power, and a manufactured war. The Empire rises by promising order while using terror, surveillance, military occupation, and superweapons.

Used future design

Star Wars feels tactile: worn ships, scratched droids, dusty settlements, brutalist bases, ceremonial temples, and cities layered over older civilizations. History is visible in the metal.
